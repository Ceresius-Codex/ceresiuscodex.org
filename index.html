Who we are

The first informatics student association at USI

Ceresius Codex was founded to fill a real gap: a community space where students of artificial intelligence, fintech, quantitative finance, and all informatics disciplines can meet, collaborate, and grow together beyond the classroom.

Named after Lugano's Latin roots, we are rooted in this city and its lake - and we look outward toward the ideas, companies, and technologies shaping tomorrow.
